# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 2, 2026**

Studio Vanilla ("Developer") values the privacy of Tiny Log ("App") users. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

**Crash and Performance Data**
- App crash logs, error messages, and device state information
- Collected via: Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)
- Purpose: Improving app stability and fixing bugs
- No personally identifiable information is included

**Advertising Data**
- Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA)
- Collected via: Google AdMob (Google LLC)
- Purpose: Serving personalized advertisements
- No longer collected after purchasing the ad removal in-app purchase

### Information We Do Not Collect

- Personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, files, or other on-device data

All user data created within the app (todos, timer records, categories, etc.) is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to external servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services

Collected data is processed through the following third-party services:

| Service | Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |

| Firebase Crashlytics | Google LLC | Crash analytics | [Link](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |

| RevenueCat | RevenueCat, Inc. | In-app purchase processing | [Link](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) |

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

- Crash logs: Retained by Firebase for 90 days, then automatically deleted
- Advertising data: Processed according to Google's advertising policies
- On-device data: All data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the app

## 4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 14 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Your Rights

- You can limit ad tracking in your device settings
- Uninstalling the app deletes all on-device data
- You can stop advertising data collection by purchasing the ad removal in-app purchase

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated through in-app notices or updates to this page.
